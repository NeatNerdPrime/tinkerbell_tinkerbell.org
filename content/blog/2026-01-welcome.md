We're excited to launch the official Tinkerbell blog! This is where we'll share project updates, technical deep-dives, best practices, and stories from our community.

## What to Expect

Here's what you can look forward to on this blog:

### Release Announcements

Stay up-to-date with the latest Tinkerbell releases. We'll cover new features, improvements, and migration guides for each version.

### Technical Deep-Dives

Learn how Tinkerbell works under the hood. We'll explore topics like:

- Workflow engine internals
- Network booting with Smee
- Hardware provisioning patterns
- Integration guides for cloud-init, Packer, and more

### Community Stories

Hear from organizations using Tinkerbell in production. Learn how they've solved real-world bare metal provisioning challenges.

### Best Practices

Get tips and tricks for running Tinkerbell effectively, from small homelab setups to large-scale data center deployments.

## Get Involved

Tinkerbell is a community-driven project, and we'd love your contributions! Here are some ways to get involved:

- **Contribute to the blog**: Have a story to share? Open a PR to add your post!
- **Join the conversation**: Find us on [Slack](https://cloud-native.slack.com/archives/C01SRB41GMT)
- **Contribute code**: Check out our [GitHub repositories](https://github.com/tinkerbell)

## Stay Connected

Follow along for updates:

- Subscribe to our [RSS feed](/blog/index.xml)
- Star us on [GitHub](https://github.com/tinkerbell/tinkerbell)
- Join the [CNCF Slack](https://cloud-native.slack.com/archives/C01SRB41GMT)
